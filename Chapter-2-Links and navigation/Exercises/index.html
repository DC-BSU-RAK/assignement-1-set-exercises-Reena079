Pirates of the Caribbean

Set Sail for an epic adventure!

Plot

Pirate Jack Sparrow goes on wild adventures to get his ship back, escape old enemies, and find magical treasures. He teams up with friends like Will and Elizabeth, fights ghost pirates and sea monsters, and tries to stay free while the navy and other pirates chase him across the seas.

Main Information

  • Release Date: July 9, 2003
  • Genre: Adventure, Action, Fantasy
  • Main Character: Captain Jack Sparrow
  • Total No. of Movies: 5
  • Box Office: $4.5 billion

Films

  1. The Curse of The Black Pearl (2003)
  2. Dead Man's Chest (2006)
  3. At World's End (2007)
  4. On Stranger Tides (2011)
  5. Dead Men Tell No Tales (2017)

Crew

  • Director:
    1. Gore Verbinski – Director of the first 3 films
    2. Rob Marshall – Director of the 4th film - Stranger Tides (2011)
    3. Joachim Rønning & Espen Sandberg – Directed the last film - Dead Men Tell No Tales (2017)
  • Producer: Jerry Bruckheimer
  • Composer: Klaus Badelt
  • Cinematographer: Dariusz Wolski
  • Production Designer: Brian Morris

Main Cast & Characters

  • Johnny Depp as Captain Jack Sparrow: The witty, unpredictable pirate and main character of the franchise.
  • Orlando Bloom as Will Turner: A skilled blacksmith-turned-pirate; later becomes captain of the Flying Dutchman.
  • Keira Knightley as Elizabeth Swann: Governor’s daughter who becomes a fierce pirate and Will's love interest.
  • Geoffrey Rush as Captain Hector Barbossa: Jack’s rival and ally, a key pirate captain with shifting loyalties.
  • Kevin McNally as Joshamee Gibbs: Jack’s loyal first mate and longtime friend.
