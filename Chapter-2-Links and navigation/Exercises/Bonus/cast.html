Pirates of the Caribbean

Set Sail for an epic adventure!

Main Cast & Characters

  • Johnny Depp as Captain Jack Sparrow: The witty, unpredictable pirate and main character of the franchise.
  • Orlando Bloom as Will Turner: A skilled blacksmith-turned-pirate; later becomes captain of the Flying Dutchman.
  • Keira Knightley as Elizabeth Swann: Governor’s daughter who becomes a fierce pirate and Will's love interest.
  • Geoffrey Rush as Captain Hector Barbossa: Jack’s rival and ally, a key pirate captain with shifting loyalties.
  • Kevin McNally as Joshamee Gibbs: Jack’s loyal first mate and longtime friend.
